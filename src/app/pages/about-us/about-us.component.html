Introduction

AGURI is crafting the next generation of heirloom pieces. Each one is designed to transition seamlessly from one wearer to the next and complement each individual’s style in a unique way. Designed and produced in New York City, AGURI’s small team of expert jewelers utilizes recycled precious metals and reclaimed or ethically mined gemstones in each piece, sparking new life into materials that may otherwise be obsolete.
